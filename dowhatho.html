About Do What Ho

"Do What Ho" is your ultimate guide to exploring Hong Kong beyond the tourist trails. We uncover the city's best-kept secrets, from hidden cafes and local markets to breathtaking hiking trails and cultural experiences.

Whether you're a local looking for new adventures or a visitor wanting to experience the real Hong Kong, we've got you covered with authentic recommendations and insider tips.

What You'll Find

Photography Trails
Capture Hong Kong's most photogenic spots, from iconic skyline views to hidden street art and everyday stories.
Local Cuisine
Discover authentic flavours at neighbourhood eateries, dai pai dongs, and modern takes on classic dishes.
Hiking & Outdoors
Explore Hong Kong's mountains and coastlines with curated routes for sunrise seekers, trail runners, and weekend wanderers.
Urban Discovery
Immerse yourself in Hong Kong's neighbourhoods, markets, and cultural pockets that tell the city's evolving story.
Hidden Cafés
Find cafes and creative spaces perfect for slow afternoons, remote work sessions, and conversations with friends.
Art & Culture
Follow art walks, pop-up galleries, and cultural events that celebrate Hong Kong's creative community.
